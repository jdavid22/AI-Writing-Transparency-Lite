About this badge

AI Writing Transparency

Why the badge?

The sender used an AI tool to help write or edit that email, and chose to tell you so. It's a small, good-faith gesture toward transparency: you know AI was involved, and roughly how much.

The badge carries none of the email's content: not the original text, not the AI's output, not any chat link. It's just a disclosure, kept deliberately small and clean.

What the effort level means

The percentage is a self-reported, plain-language estimate of how much of the wording is the AI's:

LevelRangeWhat it means
Lightly≈ 0-33%the sender did most of the writing; AI helped at the edges.
Moderately≈ 34-66%a genuine collaboration between person and AI.
Heavily≈ 67-100%the AI produced most of the wording, usually from a short prompt.

A low number isn't a brush-off

A low score doesn't mean the sender phoned it in. Some requests come up again and again, and over time a person learns exactly what works. They might drop a one-line prompt like “tell them no because security doesn't allow it, but in a nice way” and get back a polished reply in seconds.

That can look like low effort, but it's really the distillation of many past conversations and a lot of experience. A short prompt can carry years of judgment behind it, so a low number is no brush-off: it doesn't mean the sender values your question, or the interaction, any less.

How the estimate is made

It starts from a simple heuristic. If the sender wrote their own draft and the AI only edited it, the work is credited mostly to the person. If the sender gave a short prompt that the AI expanded into a much longer message, more of the wording is credited to the AI. The sender can adjust the figure by hand, so treat it as an honest gauge, not a precise measurement.

This is a good-faith, self-reported note from the sender. It is not cryptographic proof and not tamper-proof. It reflects what the sender chose to disclose. There's no tracking, no account, and no server: the badge is just a label and a link to this page.
